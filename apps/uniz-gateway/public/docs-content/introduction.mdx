---

UniZ is a university administration platform built specifically for RGUKT students, faculty, and staff. Whether you need to check your grades, request an outpass, approve a student's leave request, or publish a campus announcement — UniZ brings everything into one place, accessible from any device.

The platform is organized around the roles that make up campus life. Students get a personal dashboard for academics and mobility requests. Faculty can upload grades and attendance. Wardens, caretakers, security staff, and administrators each have dedicated workflows that keep campus operations running smoothly. Every action flows through a structured, role-based system so the right people see the right information.

UniZ is a Progressive Web App (PWA), which means you can install it directly on your phone, tablet, or desktop from your browser — no app store required. Once installed, it works like a native app with a fast, focused experience tailored to your role.
