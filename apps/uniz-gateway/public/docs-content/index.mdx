## Get started in three steps

<Steps>
  <Step title="Install the app">
    Open the UniZ portal in your browser and install it as a PWA on your device — works on iOS, Android, and desktop.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Log in with your credentials">
    Use your university-issued username and password to log in. Students use the Student Portal; faculty and staff use the Admin Portal.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start using your features">
    Students can immediately view grades and request outpasses. Admins and faculty have role-specific dashboards ready on first login.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  UniZ is built by RGUKT students, for RGUKT students. If you run into issues, use the Help & Support section inside the portal or contact your system administrator.
</Note>
